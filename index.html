tsu:つ、ツ

Stroke: 3 Left to right

Compare the letters in box-X.They are not ツ.

N:ん、ン

Stroke: 2 Top to bottomt

The First stroke:a littke angled,the 2nd strok;from top to bottom with curve.

ri:り、リ

Stroke: 2 Left to right
